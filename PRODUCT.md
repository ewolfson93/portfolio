# PRODUCT.md — Eric Wolfson Portfolio

## What this is
A standalone portfolio site positioning Eric Wolfson (PhD chemist, solo founder of My Chem Mentor)
as an **agentic systems engineer** available for AI contract and advisory work.

## Audience
Technical leads, founders, and hiring managers evaluating Eric for **AI-related contracting**:
agentic systems, multi-agent orchestration, AI automation, ML/data pipelines, RAG / agent-memory,
and the interfaces on top. They skim, then click into 1-2 case studies. They will also click through
to GitHub. The site has to read as "platform builder," not "prompt tinkerer."

## Goals
- Communicate extent + depth fast (stats, six case studies).
- Make the strongest pieces (Agentic OS, transcript pipeline, vault architecture) legible to a
  technical reviewer in under a minute each.
- Drive a contact action.

## Brand voice
- Direct, confident, technical. Casual but authoritative.
- No hype or superlatives as filler ("amazing", "game-changing", "incredible").
- No em dashes. No emojis. No "It's not just X, it's Y" parallelisms. No "No ___. Just ___."
- Plain claims backed by concrete numbers (LOC, page counts, cadence).

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)
- Generic agency landing pages with purple/violet gradients and glassmorphism.
- Hero "eyebrow chip" pill clichés stacked on a centered gradient hero.
- AI-startup template sameness: Inter everywhere, three-up feature cards with rounded icons, no point of view.
- Overlong marketing prose. This is an engineer's portfolio; the work carries it.

## Success
A reviewer leaves understanding that Eric architects and ships real AI systems, and knows how to reach him.
